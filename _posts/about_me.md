# About me

I am Maryam, a software developer with over five years of experience in the design and maintenance of diverse web applications. I am competent across the full software lifecycle, with a focus on building reliable, user-friendly solutions. I am a team player who values shared goals and enjoys supporting the professional growth of colleagues, and a practical problem-solver who enjoys technical challenges and delivering high-quality, maintainable code. I am a strong communicator in English with basic proficiency in French, and I work effectively in multicultural environments.

I am well-versed in languages and frameworks including Python, JavaScript, Django, React, Flask, GraphQL, C and C++, HTML, and CSS. My infrastructure and DevOps experience spans AWS, serverless architectures, Docker, Apptainer, Linux, and Git. For databases and data management I work with SQL and NoSQL systems, including PostgreSQL and MongoDB, and tools such as HeidiSQL. I apply Agile methodologies and tools such as Jira to keep delivery clear and collaborative.

My goal is to leverage this background to help teams ship robust, maintainable web applications and to keep growing as an engineer while contributing to products that users can rely on.
